Looking to upgrade?

Need an appliance repaired or replaced? Looking to buy a new natural gas appliance? Use our online energy referral service to find an appliance dealer or contractor in your area.

Southwest Gas is pleased to be able to provide our customers with a list of licensed natural gas heating and plumbing contractors as well as natural gas appliance dealers. When you call the contractors and/or appliance dealers, please advise them that you were referred by Southwest Gas. Also, please be sure to ask the contractor and/or appliance dealer what the convenience charge will be for the services you have requested, what their service includes, and be sure they service the area you live in with no additional travel time costs.

You can also give our Energy Specialists a call. We'll be happy to refer you to a reliable contractor or dealer and answer questions you might have about gas appliances. Call 800-654-2765.

Is the referral for work on a manufactured home?

In the state of Nevada, a contractor is required to have a special license to work on manufactured home heating systems and/or work on any gas lines after the meter. It is not required to sell or repair natural gas appliances.
